## MD-380: 人資繞道——直接找主管，留書面紀錄

**Source**: 2018-07-01, g11 family group chat, lines 47-127
**Pattern**: Edward's new company HR told him his prior 2-year work experience was "not sufficiently related" to the new role, so leave seniority would reset to zero. But after talking with colleagues, he discovers the actual work content is highly overlapping. He identifies that HR gave a fabricated reason ("她根本沒問過主管"), tried to get HR to write the decision in email ("叫她寫信就是要留紀錄，死都不寫，又不回電"), and decides: "我覺得直接跟我主管說，比較實際" — escalating to the supervisor before official hire rather than fighting HR.
**Principle**: 當行政人員給出邏輯不通的理由阻擋合理權益時：(1) 要求書面紀錄（留底）；(2) 繞過中間人直接找決策者；(3) 時間點選在「正式聘用前」——此前尚有談判空間。HR的理由不成立不等於結果不能改變，問題要找有決定權的人解決。

## MD-381: 不確定性下的預設立場——偏向保護弱方

**Source**: 2018-07-10, g11 family group chat, lines 386-498
**Pattern**: Typhoon Megi hits Taipei. Government announces 4pm stoppage (peak commute hour), causing crowds in MRT. Edward's analysis: "既然沒辦法決定就應該要放假啊" / "其實昨天就應該直接宣布半天了，下午4點根本是高峰期" / "賭贏了是神，賭輸就讓一堆人吹風回家，看起來晚上風雨就是會很大，每次都要在那邊賭". He frames the decision as: when uncertainty is high and downside asymmetric, the default should favor safety/employee side, not employer side. He also notes: "會做事的公務員平常就會做事了，會偷懶的根本不差這幾小時" — blanket policy designed around bad actors punishes everyone.
**Principle**: 在高不確定性且損益不對稱的情況下，預設立場應偏向保護弱方（員工/市民），而非等待確定再行動。「賭」的框架本身就是錯誤的——當下行風險由他人承擔時，決策者會系統性延遲。識別「誰承擔後果」是判斷決策動機的關鍵。
**Tags**: `#policy` `#decision-under-uncertainty` `#asymmetric-downside` `#institutional-incentive` `#labor-rights`
