# Twitter Thread — SOP #13: Personal Operating System

> One-claim: "Willpower is a finite resource. Your OS should never touch it."
> Target: 9 tweets. Post May 3, 2026.

---

**Tweet 1 (hook)**
If you make the same decision more than 3 times, you don't have a decision.

You have a system failure.

Here's the 5-gate OS that eliminates recurring overhead: 🧵

---

**Tweet 2 (G0)**
Gate 0: Run a 2-week decision log.

Flag every decision that appears ≥3×.

Mine: exercise (×6), lunch (×5), deep work timing (×5), coffee/tea (×4), portfolio check (×4).

All 5: SYSTEM_FAILURE.

You can't fix what you haven't audited.

---

**Tweet 3 (G1)**
Gate 1: Identify your biological peak (usually 09:00–13:00).

Hard rule: peak hours = strategy, analysis, creative work only.

If it doesn't need peak cognition, it does not happen in peak hours.

Urgency is a feeling. Not a fact.

---

**Tweet 4 (G2)**
Gate 2: Environment design before willpower.

Want to exercise? Lay out gear the night before.
Want deep work? Phone out of reach, not just silenced.
Want consistent meals? Pre-decide the rotation, not in the moment.

If willpower is required, the environment is wrong.

---

**Tweet 5 (G3)**
Gate 3: Pre-commit the top 5 system failures as permanent defaults.

Format:
"[Decision]: [Always/Never] + [Condition]"

Exercise → always yes, Mon/Wed/Fri/Sat 07:00
Lunch → rotation, ≤200 TWD cap
Portfolio check → 16:00 once daily, no earlier

Once written: remove the decision from your mental stack. It no longer exists.

---

**Tweet 6 (G4)**
Gate 4: Morning defaults routing.

First 3 routing decisions (meal, work block, exercise) must be automated before bed.

Zero-EV decisions before 10:00 = context switching tax on your most valuable hours.

The goal: wake up and execute, not wake up and decide.

---

**Tweet 7 (G5)**
Gate 5: Quarterly re-audit.

Every 90 days: re-run the decision log. New SYSTEM_FAILURES emerge as life changes.

Compounding check score = (new decisions pre-committed) / (new failures found)
≥ 0.8 → OS healthy
< 0.8 → audit cycle too slow

---

**Tweet 8 (self-test)**
Self-test:

You find yourself at 09:30 deciding whether to go to the gym.

SOP says: this decision appearing at all = SYSTEM_FAILURE.

Correct action: execute the pre-committed default. Do not re-evaluate.

Wrong action: spend 3 minutes weighing energy levels. Decide "maybe tomorrow." Repeat loop.

---

**Tweet 9 (close)**
The OS doesn't make decisions for you.

It eliminates the decisions that shouldn't exist.

5 gates: audit → protect peak → design environment → pre-commit → re-audit

Cognitive bandwidth is the scarcest resource you have. Stop spending it on lunch.
