# Twitter Thread — SOP #14: The Full-Time Switch Threshold

> Hook: "Most traders quit their job too early or never. Both are wrong. Here's the threshold calculation."
> 9 tweets. Post May 5, 2026.

---

**Tweet 1 (hook)**
Most traders quit their job too early or never.

Both are wrong.

The full-time switch isn't a feeling. It's a 5-gate threshold calculation.

Here's the SOP:

---

**Tweet 2 (G0 — edge verification)**
Gate 0: Prove your edge exists at scale first.

Requirements (all must pass):
→ ≥200 out-of-sample trades
→ Sharpe ≥ 1.0 after costs
→ Max drawdown ≤ 25%
→ MFE/MAE × √N ≥ 2.0

If Gate 0 fails, the rest is irrelevant.

A bull market is not your edge.

---

**Tweet 3 (G1 — the formula)**
Gate 1: The alpha-salary crossover calculation.

Switch threshold = Monthly salary × 1.5 × 12

The 1.5× multiplier exists because:
→ Benefits (health, pension) disappear
→ Trading income isn't linear
→ 6-month variance buffer needed

Example:
$5k/month salary → need $90k/year in trading alpha

At 30% annual return → requires $300k allocated capital minimum.

---

**Tweet 4 (G2 — evidence window)**
Gate 2: 12 consecutive months at or above threshold.

Not 12 cumulative months.
Not "12 months equivalent."
12 unbroken months.

One FAIL month resets the counter.

Why 12? Captures at least one full regime cycle — bull AND correction.

This is the filter most traders skip.

---

**Tweet 5 (G3 — cognitive prerequisite)**
Gate 3: ≥18 months of living expenses in a separate, non-trading account.

This isn't optional.

When trading capital and living expenses share the same pool:
→ A $20k drawdown feels like 3 months of rent
→ Psychological pressure contaminates every decision
→ You start managing the account for your bills, not for edge

Structure isolation first. Then switch.

---

**Tweet 6 (G4 — circuit breaker)**
Gate 4: Define the return-to-salary trigger BEFORE you quit.

Write it now:
→ Equity slope ≤ 0 over 90 days → reduce size 50%
→ 3 consecutive losing months at full size → update résumé

Knowing your exit condition makes the entry psychologically executable.

The pre-written protocol isn't pessimism. It's what makes the switch possible.

---

**Tweet 7 (switch protocol)**
When G0–G4 are green for 12 consecutive months:

Don't quit immediately.

Give 3 months notice. Trade at full allocation during overlap.

Some traders discover that removing salary urgency changes their risk appetite.

The overlap is the final filter.

---

**Tweet 8 (calibration)**
My actual numbers (2026):

Monthly baseline: ~$4,500
Switch threshold: $6,750/month = $81k/year
Required capital: $324k allocated at 25% target
Living reserve: $40k
→ **Total gate: ~$365k**

G0: paper-live validated. G2: month 1 of 12.
Earliest eligibility: 2027 Q1.

No shortcuts. The math is the map.

---

**Tweet 9 (close)**
The switch question is the wrong starting question.

The right question: "Have I completed the 5 gates?"

If yes: the switch date calculates itself.
If no: salary isn't the obstacle. Edge verification is.
