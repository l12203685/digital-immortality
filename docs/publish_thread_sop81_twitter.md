Post 1 thread per 48h. No exceptions.
Log impressions + replies after 48h.
Compute: engagement rate = replies / impressions

Below that: fix the hook, not the cadence.

Thread A gets 50 impressions → 2 replies → 1 new follower.

Velocity is the precondition. You can't compound without it.

After 10 threads you have a hook formula that works for your audience. Before 10: you're calibrating.
