---

**Tweet 4 — G0: Mental State Audit**

Gate 0: Mental State Audit (run weekly)

3 signals:

1. Decision quality rate — bad calls per week (later-judged clearly wrong)
2. Emotional reactivity events — unplanned reactive responses per week
3. Baseline mood score — 7-day morning average, before any input

Kill condition: ≥3 bad calls/week for 2 consecutive weeks → emergency protocol.

---

**Tweet 5 — G1: Derivative Scan**

Gate 1: Derivative Scan

Not just "are you functioning?" but "is function improving?"

3 derivatives:
- ΔDecision quality vs. 4-week average
- ΔEmotional reactivity vs. 4-week average
- ΔCreative output rate vs. 4-week average

Flat or declining → investigate before it becomes 2 weeks of bad calls.

---

**Tweet 6 — G2: Psychological Budget**

Gate 2: Non-Negotiable Psychological Budget

These are floors, not targets:

1. No major decisions in degraded state (any G0 Red signal)
2. ≥2h decompression after every stress event before analytical work
3. Weekly minimums: ≥7h sleep avg, ≥4 movement sessions, ≥2h unstructured solitude
4. Every reactive event gets logged — not suppressed, not analyzed in the moment

---

**Tweet 7**

That second constraint is the one people skip.

Stress event happens. You feel the urgency to respond, to fix, to move.

The protocol says: 2 hours. Non-productive. No screens, no problem-solving.

The decision you make immediately after a stress event is the most expensive one you'll ever make.

---

**Tweet 8 — G3: Quarterly Leverage Scan**

Gate 3: Quarterly Intervention Scan

Most psychological practices plateau after 90 days.

Every quarter:
1. Rank active practices by decision quality impact per time invested
2. Identify 1 candidate intervention (structural reason — not "feels good")
3. Run isolated 90-day trial
4. ΔDecision quality ≥+10% → write to permanent protocol. Otherwise archive.

---

**Tweet 9 — G4: Weekly Review**

Gate 4: Weekly Review (10 min, Sunday)

9 checks:
- Bad decisions this week?
- Reactivity events?
- 7-day mood average?
- Decision quality trending?
- Reactivity trending?
- Creative output trending?
- Sleep compliance?
- Movement compliance?
- Solitude quota met?

Log it. 10 minutes. Every week.

---

**Tweet 10 — G5: Emergency**

Gate 5: Emergency — Degraded Decision Quality

Trigger: ≥3 bad calls/week for 2 consecutive weeks.

Response:
1. Halt trading immediately
2. Income-first recovery mode — no new commitments
3. No L3 edits — lock the architecture
4. Force-run G2 floor for 14 days (sleep, movement, solitude, decompression)
5. Write 1 diagnostic entry: why did the substrate fail?

Resume only after 2 consecutive Green G0 sessions.

---

**Tweet 11**

Three failure modes most people never diagnose:

Reactive event suppression: tension accumulates without a log entry.
Decompression skipped: stress layers without discharge until reactivity spikes.
Degradation misread as normal: 2 weeks of Red looks like "life is hard right now."

Each has a gate. Each gate has a kill condition.
