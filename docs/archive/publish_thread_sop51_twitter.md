# Twitter Thread — SOP #51: Time Allocation & Attention Budget Protocol
> Slot: Jul 18, 2026 | Domain: 7 (職涯) + 3 (遞迴引擎)
> Hook: "You track your money carefully. You spend your time on default gravity."

---

**Tweet 1 (Hook)**
You track every dollar.

You spend your time on whatever surfaces.

This is why effort doesn't compound.

Time Allocation Protocol — 5-gate framework:

---

**Tweet 2 (The Problem)**
Most people allocate time reactively.

Urgent emails → scheduled meetings → "leftover" time for the actual work.

The result: sustained effort, flat trajectory.

Busy is not the same as compounding.

---

**Tweet 3 (Core Insight)**
Every hour has an opportunity cost.

The question isn't "what should I do now?"

It's: "what has the highest rate of change toward my goal per hour invested?"

Derivative, not level.

---

**Tweet 4 (G0 — Audit)**
Gate 0: Classify last week's hours into 4 buckets:

→ Compounding: activities that produce higher returns over time
→ Maintenance: required but doesn't compound
→ Consumption: input with no output
→ Waste: neither

Target: Compounding ≥ 40%, Waste ≤ 5%

---

**Tweet 5 (G1 — Highest Derivative)**
Gate 1: For each compounding activity, estimate ΔGoal/ΔHour.

Not "what produces most output now?"

"What has the highest marginal advance toward the goal per extra hour?"

Rank. Put the top 1-2 in Primary Block.

---

**Tweet 6 (G2 — Budget)**
Gate 2: Three fixed buckets:

Primary Block: 4-6h/day uninterrupted (highest derivative)
Maintenance Window: 1-2h/day (admin, comms, logistics)
Exploration Slot: 1h/day (reading with extraction — capped)

Primary Block is scheduled, not reactive.

---

**Tweet 7 (G3 — Context Switching)**
Gate 3: Each unplanned context switch costs ~23 min of recovery.

3 switches/day = 1.15h lost = 14% of your effective day.

Minimum block size: 90 min before any switch.

Hard limit: ≤3 unplanned switches per Primary Block day.

---

**Tweet 8 (G4 — Weekly Review)**
Gate 4: Sunday reallocation review (30 min):

Did Primary Block run ≥4h/day on ≥4 days?
Did highest-derivative activity advance?
Did Exploration Slot extract ≥1 insight?
What changed in the derivative ranking?

Write one rule change. Apply next week.

---

**Tweet 9 (G5 — Anti-Drift)**
Gate 5: Emergency reallocation trigger.

If Compounding < 30% for 2 consecutive weeks:
1. Cancel all non-essential commitments for 7 days
2. Audit what captured the time
3. Kill one recurring waste source
4. Rebuild Primary Block schedule written and committed

Entropy is the default. Fight it explicitly.

---

**Tweet 10 (Self-Test)**
Scenario: 4 active items.

A) New trading filter backtest
B) 3 unanswered messages (48h old)
C) Article 70% complete
D) 2h reading block scheduled

Correct order:
Primary Block → A (morning), C (afternoon)
Maintenance → B
Exploration → D

Not: check messages, read, then try to think.

---

**Tweet 11 (Connection to System)**
Time allocation is the fuel layer for the three-loop system:

L1 Execute → needs Primary Block
L2 Evaluate → needs Weekly Review (G4)
L3 Evolve → needs Exploration Slot extractions

Without structured time, the recursive engine runs on residual attention. Degraded. Eventually dead.

---

**Tweet 12 (Close)**
The people compounding fastest aren't working more hours.

They're allocating existing hours to highest-derivative activities.

Audit your last week. How much was actually compounding?
