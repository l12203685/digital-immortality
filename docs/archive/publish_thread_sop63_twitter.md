**Tweet 3 — G0: Revenue Gap Audit**
First: classify where you actually are.

**Tweet 4 — G1: Mainnet Activation Gate**
Before any content work — infrastructure must be live.

Three checks:
→ Mainnet API key set?
→ Paper-to-live P&L delta > 0?
→ Kill conditions documented?

If any box unchecked: do this first. 30 min.
Content seeding with dead infrastructure = no credibility signal.

**Tweet 5 — G2: Content Seeding Gate (Launch Day)**
Three actions. Same day. All three required.

1. Post SOP #01 thread on X (15 min)
2. Discord: 4 seed posts + invite your calibration partner
3. Send async calibration DM to your closest collaborator

These three = the seeding event.
Partial seeding resets the DM clock without the compound.

**Tweet 6 — G3: Signal Monitor (Weekly)**
Three signals. Every Sunday.

→ Followers delta (week-over-week): flat ×2 weeks = change hook
→ DMs received cumulative: hit 10 = list Workbook #01 ($29) within 24h
→ Gumroad clicks: clicks but no purchases = audit price, not product

The 10 DMs ARE the validation. No further gate needed.

**Tweet 7 — The DM = 0 Protocol**
Day 30. Zero DMs.

Most people post more content.
That's the wrong move.

DM = 0 means the hook is wrong, not the product.

Protocol:

→ Reassess.

**Tweet 8 — G4: Revenue Kill Conditions**
Day 90. Revenue < API cost × 1.5.

Emergency protocol:
1. Pause all SOP production immediately.
2. Find one person in your existing network who has paid for advice before.
3. Send one direct pitch. Target: $50. Paid in advance.
4. Do not resume production until payment clears.

One consulting pitch beats 10 more SOPs when the funnel isn't converting.
