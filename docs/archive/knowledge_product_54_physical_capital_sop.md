# SOP #54 — Physical Capital & Body Investment Protocol

> Domain: 5 (身體健康 Physical Capital)
> Created: 2026-04-09T UTC (cycle 215)
> DNA anchors: MD-89 (單日時限) / MD-144 (監控帶寬是上限) / MD-12 (看導數不看水平) / MD-48 (知識=時間密度乘積) / MD-67 (現金流=買時間)

---

## One-Claim Insight

**Physical capital is the only non-replicable asset.** Money, skills, and even time can be partially recovered. Your body — specifically its energy output ceiling, cognitive peak quality, and injury-free range of motion — cannot be rebuilt after neglect accumulates. Most people treat their body as a cost center (inputs to minimize) not a capital account (investment with compounding return). The correct model: body investment → higher cognitive ceiling → more derivative per hour → accelerated compounding on everything else.

---

## 5-Gate Framework

### G0: Physical Capital Audit

**Question**: What is the current state of my physical capital account?

Three signals, daily check (2 min):
1. **Energy baseline**: 1–10 scale on waking. Trend over 5 days (not single point).
2. **Cognitive peak quality**: Did peak cognitive window (first 3–4h after wake) hit L3 decisions, or was it wasted on maintenance?
3. **Body signal log**: Any recurring signal (tension, pain, fatigue pattern, digestion issue)?

**Kill condition**: 3 consecutive days Energy ≤ 4 OR cognitive peak <50% utilized → G5.

---

### G1: Physical Derivative Scan

**Question**: Is physical capital trending up, flat, or declining?

Track 3 derivatives (not absolute levels):
- **ΔEnergy/week**: +1 or more = compounding; flat = maintenance; −1 or more = erosion
- **ΔInjury frequency**: New recurring signals appearing? Or resolving?
- **ΔCognitive peak quality**: Peak window improving, stable, or shrinking?

**Rule (MD-12)**: Do not manage by how you feel today. Manage by the slope of the last 5 days.

**Threshold**: If any derivative is negative for 2 consecutive weeks → G4 (structural review), not willpower patch.

---

### G2: Non-Negotiable Physical Investment Budget

**Minimum viable physical investment (non-negotiable, like SOP #52 sleep budget):**

| Category | Minimum | Rationale |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| Sleep | 7–8h in bed (SOP #52) | Energy baseline requires this as foundation |
| Movement | 20–30 min/day (any intensity) | Breaks sedentary load, resets focus for next cognitive block |
| Nutrition timing | No >6h fast during peak cognitive window | Glucose regulation for L3 decision quality |
| Hydration | Active check at 3 fixed points (wake/midday/evening) | Dehydration degrades cognitive performance before thirst |

**Rule**: These are scheduled constraints. Deadlines reschedule. Physical budget does not.

**Edge case**: If a deadline compresses all 4 — movement is the most recoverable (skip once). Sleep is non-negotiable (SOP #52 G2 applies).

---

### G3: Physical Leverage Scan

**Question**: Which single physical input has the highest derivative on cognitive output?

Run this quarterly, not weekly. Physical leverage points are slow-changing:

1. List current physical routines (sleep, exercise, nutrition, hydration, recovery habits)
2. Estimate which single change would most increase cognitive peak quality or energy ceiling
3. Run 2-week trial of that single change (isolation, not stack)
4. Measure G0 and G1 before and after
5. If ΔEnergy ≥ +1 or ΔCognitive peak ≥ +20% → write to protocol; kill old default

**Anti-pattern**: Stacking multiple health interventions simultaneously. Unattributable results. Run isolated trials.

---

### G4: Weekly Physical Review

**Sunday, 10 min. Three questions:**

1. Did the G2 budget run this week? (Y/N per day — track, don't estimate)
2. What is the derivative direction (G1)? Improving / flat / declining?
3. One structural cause if declining: behavioral (choice), environmental (friction too high), or systemic (load mismatched to capacity)?

**Action by cause**:
- Behavioral → adjust scheduling (not willpower — friction is the root)
- Environmental → reduce friction on the minimum viable input
- Systemic → reduce total load (G5 if severe) or rebalance primary blocks

---

### G5: Physical Capital Emergency

**Trigger**: Energy baseline ≤ 4 × 3 days OR injury that blocks movement OR cognitive peak quality < 30% × 3 consecutive days.

**Protocol**:
1. Physical capital becomes highest-derivative activity — re-rank above all Branch work
2. Reduce schedule to: sleep + 1 Primary Block only (stop secondary work streams)
3. Emergency recovery target: +2 on Energy baseline within 5 days
4. No L3 DNA edits during emergency (degraded cognitive state = error-prone evolution)
5. Cause root-lock: after recovery, identify 1 structural cause and implement 1 friction reduction before resuming full schedule

**Why no L3 edits**: Cognitive state during emergency does not meet G4 anti-drift gate (SOP #50). Low-quality rule updates corrupt the kernel. Wait until G0 ≥ 6 × 2 days.

## Integration with Three-Layer Loop

| Layer | Physical Capital Hook |
|-------|-----------------------|
| L1 Execute | Run G2 budget daily (movement/sleep/nutrition/hydration) |
| L2 Evaluate | G0 audit + G1 derivative check (2 min/day, structured review Sunday) |
| L3 Evolve | G3 leverage scan quarterly → write new physical protocol if ΔEnergy ≥ threshold |

Physical capital is infrastructure for all three layers. L3 evolution quality degrades if L1 physical budget is not met.

## Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Why it fails |
|---------|-------------|
| "I'll recover on weekends" | Chronic debt doesn't clear in 2 days; derivative stays negative |
| Stacking interventions | Can't attribute improvement to any single lever |
| Managing by single-point feeling | Misses structural trends; high variance day-to-day |
| Skipping G2 for deadlines | Reduces cognitive peak → same deadline takes longer → net loss |
| L3 edits during G5 | Degraded state corrupts kernel rules — wait for recovery |

## Summary

Physical capital = the only non-replicable asset. Manage it like a capital account:
- G0: Daily 2-min audit (3 signals)
- G1: Derivative scan (slope of 5 days, not today)
- G2: Non-negotiable daily investment (sleep + movement + nutrition timing + hydration)
- G3: Quarterly leverage scan (one isolated trial at a time)
- G4: Weekly review (10 min Sunday — Y/N tracking + cause classification)
- G5: Emergency — re-rank body above all branch work until recovery

Invest in physical capital: higher ceiling → more derivative per hour → everything else compounds faster.
