# Twitter Thread — SOP #54: Physical Capital Protocol

> Domain: 5 (身體健康)
> Slot: Jul 24, 2026
> Created: 2026-04-09T UTC (cycle 215)

---

**HOOK (Tweet 1)**
You can rebuild savings.
You can re-learn skills.
You cannot rebuild a body that's been running on debt for 5 years.

Most people treat their body like a cost center.
It's actually your only non-replicable asset.

5-gate Physical Capital Protocol. 🧵

---

**Tweet 2**
The wrong mental model: "health = discipline"

The right model: "body = capital account"

Capital accounts compound when invested.
They erode when neglected.
The slope matters more than today's balance.

---

**Tweet 3**
G0: Daily 2-min Physical Audit

Three signals:
→ Energy baseline (1–10 on wake)
→ Cognitive peak quality (did your best window go to L3 work or maintenance?)
→ Body signal log (any recurring tension/fatigue pattern?)

Kill condition: 3 consecutive days ≤4 energy → Emergency (G5)

---

**Tweet 4**
G1: Derivative Scan

Don't ask "how do I feel today?"
Ask: "what is the slope of the last 5 days?"

Track 3 derivatives:
→ ΔEnergy/week
→ ΔInjury frequency
→ ΔCognitive peak quality

If any derivative is negative for 2 consecutive weeks → structural cause, not willpower.

---

**Tweet 5**
G2: Non-Negotiable Physical Investment Budget

4 minimums. Not goals — hard constraints:
→ 7–8h sleep (SOP #52 applies)
→ 20–30 min movement/day
→ No >6h fast during peak cognitive window
→ Hydration check at 3 fixed points (wake/midday/evening)

Deadlines reschedule. This budget does not.

---

**Tweet 6**
Why movement is first to cut:

Movement is the most recoverable minimum (skip once, OK).
Sleep is not (SOP #52).
Nutrition timing and hydration are invisible until they're not.

Edge case: if you must compress — skip movement first, never sleep.

---

**Tweet 7**
G3: Quarterly Physical Leverage Scan

Which single input has the highest derivative on your cognitive output?

Run isolated 2-week trials.
Not stacked interventions — you can't attribute compounded changes.

If ΔEnergy ≥ +1 or ΔCognitive peak ≥ +20% → write it to protocol.

---

**Tweet 8**
The anti-pattern: stacking 5 interventions simultaneously.

You lose 2kg, sleep better, feel more focused.
Which of the 5 caused it?

You don't know.
Next year you're back to 0 and you don't know why.

Isolated trials. One lever at a time.

---

**Tweet 9**
G4: Weekly Physical Review (Sunday, 10 min)

Three questions:
1. Did G2 budget run this week? (Y/N per day)
2. What's the derivative direction? (G1 check)
3. If declining — behavioral, environmental, or systemic?

Action by cause:
→ Behavioral: fix scheduling (not willpower)
→ Environmental: reduce friction
→ Systemic: reduce total load

---

**Tweet 10**
G5: Physical Capital Emergency

Trigger: Energy ≤4 × 3 days OR injury that blocks movement OR cognitive peak <30% × 3 days.

Protocol:
→ Body becomes highest-derivative activity
→ Reduce to sleep + 1 Primary Block
→ Target: +2 energy baseline in 5 days
→ No DNA/rule edits until recovery

Why no rule edits: degraded cognitive state = corrupted kernel. Wait.

---

**Tweet 11**
Physical capital → cognitive ceiling → derivative per hour → everything else compounds faster.

The chain works in reverse too:
Neglect body → lower ceiling → less derivative per hour → everything takes longer.

It's not a lifestyle choice. It's a multiplier on all other investments.
