**Tweet 1 (Hook)**

**Tweet 2**
Most people optimize the strategy.

I optimize the regime classification.

Regime first. Strategy second.

The best trend-following system has NEGATIVE expectancy in a ranging market.

---

**Tweet 3**
Gate 0: Define 3 regimes before anything runs.

• Trending: ATR > 30-day ATR average → run breakout/momentum
• Ranging: ATR < 30-day ATR average → run mean-reversion
• Event: scheduled catalyst → reduce leverage 50%, period.

ATR is your regime speedometer.

---

**Tweet 4**
Why ATR and not price?

Price tells you direction.
ATR tells you whether the market is *moving at all*.

A strategy that needs movement to make money needs ATR above baseline.

Without movement, you're paying spread and commissions to go nowhere.

---

**Tweet 5**
Gate 1: ATR rotation signal

ATR(14) today vs rolling 30-day average of ATR(14)

Above average → trending regime, activate trend pool
Below average → ranging regime, activate mean-reversion pool

Simple. Objective. No judgment calls.

---

**Tweet 6**
Gate 2: Rolling OOS reality check

Here's the trap: you think the strategy "works now" because it worked last week.

That's recency bias masquerading as regime adaptation.

Fix: minimum 90-trade OOS window + ATR signal consistent for ≥5 days before confirming regime shift.

---

**Tweet 7**
Gate 3: Strategy pool long/short audit

Every quarter, count:
• How many of your strategies can go SHORT?

Target: ≥30% of pool is short-capable.

If you can only make money in up markets, you don't have a trading system.

You have levered beta with extra steps.

---

**Tweet 8**
Gate 4: Equity curve = portfolio regime detector

• Equity within 5% of ATH → Normal (full leverage)
• 5–15% drawdown → Warning (cut leverage 50%, pause weakest strategy)
• >15% drawdown → Stop (all strategies off, paper only)

Your equity curve tells you the truth faster than any indicator.

---

**Tweet 9**
Gate 5: Rotation execution

DO NOT close all positions when regime shifts.

1. Stop opening new trades in the deactivated strategy
2. Let existing trades finish their natural exit
3. Activate new strategy set for the next signal only
4. Log the rotation (timestamp, ATR values, equity state)

Rotation is slow. One crossover ≠ confirmed shift.

The signal isn't "what will happen."
