## What This Closes

Gap: **SOP #102 declares readiness. Nothing operationalizes the decision to post.**

SOP #102 (Pre-Launch Readiness Audit) confirms all 6 gates are GREEN.  
SOP #83 (Daily Posting Ritual) runs the ongoing daily cycle.  
Neither covers the ignition moment itself — the 30-minute irreversible commit from "I'm ready" to "SOP #01 is live."

Without this protocol, the launch moment becomes a decision-paralysis trap:  
"I should post... but let me review one more time... let me check the thread again..."  
Readiness theater: auditing instead of launching.

---

## Protocol

### G0 — Final Gate Check (2 min)

Run SOP #102. If all 6 gates GREEN: proceed.  
If any gate RED: fix it (max 1 fix allowed — if still RED, launch anyway; perfect launch = no launch).

Decision rule: **G0 is pass/fail, not a reason to delay.**

---

### G1 — Set the Clock (1 min)

Write down: "I am posting SOP #01 at [current time + 15 min]."  
This is a commitment device. The clock runs. No more reviewing.

---

### G2 — Copy-Paste Execute (5 min)

Open `docs/publish_thread_sop01_twitter.md`.  
Copy tweet 1. Paste. Post.  
Copy tweet 2. Reply to tweet 1. Post.  
Continue until thread complete.

No editing. No improvements. The thread was written. Trust the prep.

---

### G3 — Pin and Signal (2 min)

Pin the SOP #01 thread to profile.  
Update bio if needed (points to pinned thread or product page).  
Post "just dropped [SOP #01 link] — [1-line hook]" as a standalone tweet.

---

### G4 — Start M1 Clock (1 min)

Open SOP #83 (Daily Posting Ritual).  
Mark today as Day 1.  
Schedule Day 2 post in queue.

M1 milestone = 10 DMs within 7 days. SOP #90 tracking starts now.

---

### G5 — Debrief Log (2 min)

Write 2 sentences to `staging/session_state.md`:
1. "Launched SOP #01 at [timestamp]."
2. "Day 1 action taken: [what you did beyond the post]."

This is the handoff to the daily ritual.

---

## Decision Rules

| Situation | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| Thread needs minor edits | Post it as-is. Edit later as a reply. |
| Engagement is low Day 1 | Expected. SOP #83 runs for 7 days before judgment. |
| Anxiety spike before posting | Run G1 clock. The decision is already made. |
| "Maybe I should wait until..." | No. SOP #102 already answered this. |

---

## Anti-Patterns

- **Extended readiness audit**: Doing SOP #102 a second time instead of launching.
- **Thread perfectionism**: Editing the launch thread at T-5 min. The thread was written cold. It's correct.
- **Soft launch**: Posting with "just sharing a thought..." framing. Own it.
