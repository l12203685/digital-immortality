# Twitter Thread — SOP #40: Information Asymmetry Edge Protocol

**Hook tweet:**
Most people trade ideas. A few trade edges.

The difference: one has a structural reason to be right.

A framework for finding yours — thread: 🧵

---

**Tweet 2:**
Edge exists in exactly 4 forms:

A. Access — data others don't see
B. Interpretation — same data, non-consensus framework
C. Execution — same info, faster/more disciplined
D. Behavioral — same info, others won't act (structure, emotion, constraint)

If you can't point to one of these → you don't have an edge.

---

**Tweet 3:**
The #1 mistake: confusing a thesis with an edge.

"I think BTC will go up" is a thesis.
"I have a quantitative signal that fires when institutional positioning diverges from retail" is an edge.

Theses are opinions. Edges are structural.

---

**Tweet 4:**
All edges decay. The question is: how fast?

Access edge → decays when data goes public
Interpretation edge → decays when framework gets mainstream
Behavioral edge → slowest decay (crowd habits change slowly)

Before sizing: ask "how long is this edge valid?"

Your position duration should match your edge duration.

---

**Tweet 5:**
Adversarial pressure test (mandatory before any large bet):

1. Who is on the other side?
2. Why are they wrong — and what do THEY know that you don't?
3. What if YOU'RE the one with the information gap?

Can't answer Q2 → reduce size by 50%.
Q3 is plausible and irreversible → don't act.

---

**Tweet 6:**
Sizing formula:

Position = base_unit × confidence × duration_multiplier

confidence: 1.0 (edge clearly identified), 0.5 (partial), 0.0 (none)
duration: 2.0 (>6 months), 1.0 (weeks–months), 0.3 (<1 week)

Max cap: never exceed 2× base regardless of confidence. Tail risk doesn't care about your edge score.

---

**Tweet 7:**
Population exploit is the highest-duration edge.

When >70% of market participants hold the same direction for a structural reason (not just momentum):
→ contrarian signal upgrades to Type D

But you NEED the structural reason. "Everyone is long" alone is not an edge. "Everyone is long because of forced institutional FOMO with a hard redemption deadline" — that's an edge.

---

**Tweet 8:**
The edge log:

Every active position gets:
- Asymmetry type (A/B/C/D)
- One-sentence statement: "I have an edge because [X] while the market doesn't know/won't act on [Y]"
- Valid until: [date or trigger condition]
- Exit trigger: [what signals decay]

Quarterly review: if you can't defend the statement → close and restart.

---

**Tweet 9:**
Anti-patterns that kill traders and careers:

→ "Gut feeling" — not an asymmetry
→ Holding a decayed edge — was valid 6 months ago, still in the trade
→ Undercounting competition — assuming you're the only one who found this
→ Contrarianism without structure — being against the crowd for its own sake

Being contrarian is not an edge. Having a structural reason the crowd is anchored wrong — that's the edge.
