**What is an organism DNA?**

It's not a personality profile. It's a *decision machine*.

The goal: given scenario X, the document should produce the same answer you would — even when you're not in the room.

---

**Structure** (what's in mine, after 18 months):

1. **Core principles** (~300+ extracted from 10 years of past decisions)
2. **Decision labels** — how I label decisions before acting: PASS / TAKE / CONDITIONAL / WAIT
3. **Domain rules** — what changes by domain (trading vs career vs social)
4. **Known failure modes** — anti-patterns I've actually demonstrated, not invented
5. **Behavioral patterns** — what changes under pressure, under scarcity, under novelty

---

**A real fragment** (from §2 Decision Framework):

```
Before any decision:
1. What is the derivative? (change rate, not current state)
2. Where is the information asymmetry?
3. What's the meta-strategy layer? (is this a strategy-level or meta-strategy-level question?)
4. Action: PASS if no edge. TAKE if edge is clear. CONDITIONAL if depends on named condition.
```

**From §6 Domain Rules — Trading:**
```
- Never enter a position without knowing the exit condition before entry
- Regime matters more than signal: trending → momentum; mean-reverting → mean-reversion
- Size for the bad scenario, not the expected one
- Paper trading = evidence; gut = prior; conflict → trust paper
```

**From §5 Known Failure Modes:**
```
- Alignment theater: restating feedback without changing behavior
- Conscious idle: labeling laziness as strategic inaction
- Priority inversion: building when the person asked "are you learning me?"
- Asking known questions: DNA has the answer; asking anyway = wasted cycle
```

---

**How to build one:**

You don't start with a blank form. You start with past decisions.

Pick 5 decisions you made in the last year that surprised *you* — where you deviated from what you thought you'd do. Extract the rule that explains the deviation. That's your real DNA.

"I thought I'd negotiate harder but I didn't" → rule: "Relationship preservation overrides maximum EV extraction in long-tenure contexts."

The document is your decision log, compressed into principles.
